---
title: Email Marketing
---

A common founder stack is:

Newsletter: Beehiiv
Marketing Emails: Mailchimp or MailerLite
Creator Business: ConvertKit
Transactional Emails: Resend
Enterprise Email Delivery: SendGrid
Cold Outreach: Instantly


| Tool | Description | Link |
|------|-------------|------|
| Mailchimp | Most popular email marketing platform for newsletters and campaigns | https://mailchimp.com/ |
| Beehiiv | Modern newsletter platform with built-in monetization and growth tools | https://www.beehiiv.com/ |
| ConvertKit | Email marketing platform for creators, indie founders, and online businesses | https://kit.com/ |
| MailerLite | Simple and affordable email marketing with a generous free plan | https://www.mailerlite.com/ |
| Resend | Developer-focused transactional email platform with modern APIs | https://resend.com/ |
| SendGrid | Enterprise-grade transactional and marketing email delivery platform | https://sendgrid.com/ |
| Instantly | Cold email outreach and email marketing platform with Unibox and inbox management | https://instantly.ai/ |
