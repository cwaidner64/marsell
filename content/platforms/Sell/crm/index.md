Common founder stack:

| Stage                           | Recommended CRM      |
| ------------------------------- | -------------------- |
| Pre-PMF / Solo Founder          | Attio                |
| Early Revenue (<$1M ARR)        | Attio or HubSpot CRM |
| Sales Team Growing              | HubSpot CRM or Close |
| Heavy Outbound                  | Close                |
| Enterprise Customization        | Zoho CRM             |
| Traditional Pipeline Management | Pipedrive            |



| Tool | Description | Link |
|------|-------------|------|
| HubSpot CRM | Most widely used free CRM, great for early-stage startups | https://www.hubspot.com/products/crm |
| Attio | Modern, flexible CRM built for startups with powerful relationship management and automation | https://attio.com/ |
| Pipedrive | Sales-focused CRM designed to help teams close deals faster | https://www.pipedrive.com/ |
| Close | CRM built for inside sales teams and B2B startups | https://www.close.com/ |
| Zoho CRM | Affordable and highly customizable CRM platform | https://www.zoho.com/crm/ |
| Apollo.io | Outbound prospecting platform with a database of 275M+ contacts | https://www.apollo.io/ |
| Clay | Data enrichment and AI-powered outbound automation platform | https://www.clay.com/ |
