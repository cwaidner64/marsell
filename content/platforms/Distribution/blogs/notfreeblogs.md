Recommendations:

Solo Founder: Beehiiv
Indie Hacker: Ghost
Startup Marketing Site: Framer
SEO-Focused SaaS: WordPress.org
Developer Founder: Hashnode Pro
Content-Led Growth: Beehiiv + Ghost


| Num | Name | Link | Caveat |
|:---:|------|------|------|
| 1 | Beehiiv | https://www.beehiiv.com/ | Amazing free tier, but advanced growth, automation, and monetization features require a paid plan |
| 2 | Ghost Pro | https://ghost.org/ | One of the best publishing platforms, but requires a paid subscription for hosted plans |
| 3 | WordPress.org | https://wordpress.org/ | Software is free, but hosting, themes, and plugins often incur costs |
| 4 | Webflow CMS | https://webflow.com/ | Powerful visual website builder with excellent blogging and SEO capabilities, but requires a paid CMS plan |
| 5 | Framer | https://www.framer.com/ | Modern website builder with blogging support; paid plans required for serious publishing |
| 6 | HubSpot CMS | https://www.hubspot.com/products/cms | Excellent for content marketing and SEO, but expensive compared to alternatives |
| 7 | Contentful | https://www.contentful.com/ | Headless CMS for scaling content operations; requires technical setup and paid plans |
| 8 | Sanity | https://www.sanity.io/ | Developer-friendly headless CMS with generous free tier but often requires paid usage at scale |
| 9 | Storyblok | https://www.storyblok.com/ | Headless CMS with visual editing; pricing increases as content grows |
| 10 | ButterCMS | https://buttercms.com/ | API-first blogging and content management platform for developers |
| 11 | Hashnode Pro | https://hashnode.com/ | Paid features for custom branding, analytics, and publication management |
| 12 | Feather | https://feather.so/ | Turns Notion into a blog with custom domains and SEO features |
| 13 | Typedream | https://typedream.com/ | No-code website and blogging platform built for startups and creators |
| 14 | Letterdrop | https://letterdrop.com/ | B2B SEO blogging and content distribution platform |
| 15 | Outstatic | https://outstatic.com/ | Git-based CMS designed for Next.js and content-driven websites |
