---
title: Customer Support
---

Note, this is when you have customers
Pre-PMF: Crisp
Early Revenue: Intercom
Growing Team: Zendesk or Freshdesk
B2B SaaS Focus: Plain


| Tool | Description | Link |
|------|-------------|------|
| Intercom | In-app messaging, onboarding, customer engagement, and support platform | https://www.intercom.com/ |
| Zendesk | Full-featured customer support, ticketing, and help desk suite | https://www.zendesk.com/ |
| Freshdesk | Affordable customer support and ticketing platform, often considered a Zendesk alternative | https://www.freshworks.com/freshdesk/ |
| Plain | Modern B2B SaaS support platform built around Slack, email, and customer conversations | https://www.plain.com/ |
| Crisp | Live chat, customer messaging, and support platform with a generous free plan | https://crisp.chat/ |
