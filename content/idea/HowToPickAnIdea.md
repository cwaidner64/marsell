Before we jump in, the idea needs to be conceputally sound.

There's three main components everyone must consider when picking an idea:
1. Business Model [[BusinessModel]]
2. Market [[Market]]
3. Concept [[Concept]]

All three are esstainial to a well functioning idea, in order to build and grow a startup, having an idea of the direction of the company is critical to form an hypothesis of how the market will turn out to be.
